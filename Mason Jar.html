Mason Jar Restaurant

This was an amazing opportunity, and I enjoyed it too much. I really look forward to doing more work like this for future clients. I was asked to come up with a whole brand identity for a restaurant that would feature live music outside. They had a really nice atmosphere planned out, simple menu and nice sipping drinks.

Unfortunately, the owners were unable to continue with the restaurant and had to stop the whole project. I did however get paid for my design work. But I was more interested in the fact that, I would have my own design on everything that restaurant would have facilitated. That is what I find so intoxicating about design.
